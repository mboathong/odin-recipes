Creamy Odin's Italian Sauce

This creamy Italian dressing is easy to make and wonderfully thick!

This dressing is simple to make—all you have to do is blend everything together.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • ½ small onion
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • ¾ teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • ¼ teaspoon garlic powder
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ⅛ teaspoon ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Blend mayonnaise, onion, vinegar, sugar, Italian seasoning, garlic powder, salt, and pepper in a blender until smooth.
